How it works

Three steps.
Zero confusion.

From QR scan to personal receipt, the entire flow takes under 60 seconds.

01
📱
Scan & Download

Each diner scans the table QR code. First-timers are directed to download the BillEat app, sign up with Google, Apple, or anonymously. Return visits open the app directly to your table.

02
🍽️
Select Your Items

The full table bill appears. Each diner taps what they ordered, including split quantities for shared dishes.

03
✅
Pay Your Share

Confirm your items and your personal receipt is generated instantly. The staff gets a notification, a waiter comes to you directly to process your payment. No waiting, no confusion.
